Our Process

Buying land through Legacy Land Company is simple, transparent, and personalized. Here's what to expect:

📩

1. Make an Inquiry

Reach out through our website, message us on Facebook, or contact an agent directly. We're ready to help.

📞

2. Schedule a Call or Zoom

We’ll walk you through the property details, answer questions, and confirm it's the right fit.

📝

3. Sign the Contract

We send you the purchase agreement electronically. Once signed, your land is officially reserved.

💳

4. Complete Checkout

Upload your government-issued ID and make your first payment through our secure checkout page.

🏢

5. Title Company Coordination

We work with a trusted title company who will handle official documentation and monthly payments going forward.
